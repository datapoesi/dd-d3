The Pregnant Timetraveller's Guide to Names

Every year, thousands of pregnant women travel uni-directionally back in time – either by choice or by mishap. This interactive guide was created to help those women name their newborn companions. To help them – you – pick a name that is not only a name of its time, but also a popular name of its time. A name that allows your ChronoBaby™ to conform to its environment. A name that will not ostracize you and the child – or worse, reveal you as a timetraveller. Because that is, after all, one of the most important rules to follow as a responsible timetraveller: to stay low, play it safe, and to not do anything that would raise attention to your time-travel shenanigans.
